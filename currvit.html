Dr Thomas Goodge

Email   Github   Scholar   LinkedIn  

I am a Research Associate in the Glasgow Interactive Systems Group (GIST) within the School of Computing Science (SoCS) at the University of Glasgow. My main research interests lie in the interactions of Cognitive Psychology and Human-Computer Interaction; in particular, evaluating visual attention and decision making while engaged with ubiquitous mixed reality and artificial intelligence technologies. This ranges from investigating visual salience and perceptual processes in mixed reality, to the cognitive mechanisms used in assessing and understanding the world when using LLM/VLMs, as well as the decision making and risk appraisals that follow.
